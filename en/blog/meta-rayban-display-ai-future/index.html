    Meta Ray-Ban Display Announcement: The Dawn of AI Agent Era Starting at $799¶

    Meta Ray-Ban Display was announced on September 17, 2025, poised to revolutionize the smart glasses market. Furthermore, this product opens a new era of consumer-oriented display-equipped smart glasses with a strategic price point of $799.

    Groundbreaking Announcement: Meta Ray-Ban Display Overview¶

    At Meta Connect 2025, Mark Zuckerberg officially announced the Meta Ray-Ban Display. Therefore, these smart glasses represent not just a wearable device, but the first step toward a next-generation computing platform.

    Pricing and Release Schedule¶

    The Meta Ray-Ban Display is priced at $799, launching in select US stores starting September 30, 2025. Additionally, expansion to Canada, France, Italy, and the UK is planned for early 2026.

    This pricing is higher compared to the existing Ray-Ban Meta ($379), but it's reasonable considering the display functionality. Specifically, Meta's official website clearly demonstrates the intention to achieve market penetration through a staged pricing strategy.

    Display Feature Characteristics¶

    The high-resolution full-color display mounted on the right lens is the core functionality of the Meta Ray-Ban Display. Specifically, this display features a privacy-focused design that only the wearer can see.

    Beyond displaying messages from WhatsApp, Messenger, and Instagram, it also includes video calling and navigation features. Moreover, Meta AI's visual search result display realizes smartphone's primary functions right before your eyes.

    Revolutionary Interface Enabled by Neural Band¶

    The true innovation of the Meta Ray-Ban Display lies in the Neural Band controller. This enables a completely new interaction experience, freed from traditional touchscreen or button operations.

    EMG Technology Mechanism¶

    Neural Band uses EMG (electromyography) technology to detect signals sent from the brain to the hands. For example, you can operate the Meta Ray-Ban Display's display with just subtle tap gestures between thumb and index finger.

    This technology eliminates the need to take out your smartphone from your pocket, allowing discreet access to information. According to detailed EMG technology explanations, this approach has the potential to enable even more advanced operations in the future.

    Practical Operation Experience¶

    Neural Band boasts an 18-hour battery life and features waterproof specifications for active use. More importantly, its simple design similar to Fitbit enables daily wear.

    Zuckerberg described this technology as "the next chapter in computing history." Indeed, when combined with Meta Ray-Ban Display, it realizes a new experience where you can check and operate information without others noticing.

    Fusion of Meta Ray-Ban Display and AI Agents¶

    Through Live AI functionality, Meta Ray-Ban Display becomes more than just glasses with a display. In other words, it realizes true contextual AI that can understand what the wearer sees, record what they hear, and provide assistance later.

    Live AI Feature Details¶

    Meta Ray-Ban Display's AI capabilities cover a wide range from real-time translation to visual inquiries. For example, during overseas travel, you can instantly translate signs or conversations, or ask questions about what you're seeing.

    Furthermore, the contextual memory function allows you to reference things you've seen in the past. This way, proactive assistance enables automatic information provision according to the situation.

    Future Prospects: Fusion of Metaverse and Reality¶

    Meta positions the Meta Ray-Ban Display as "the next major personal computing platform." The product roadmap from the current Ray-Ban Meta to this Display, and then to the experimental Orion AR glasses, demonstrates a strategy to gradually integrate the AR world into reality.

    Integration of Meta Horizon Studio and Hyperscape technology enables seamless fusion of the physical and digital worlds. In conclusion, Meta Ray-Ban Display will define a new relationship with AI agents and serve as an important step toward the next-generation platform replacing smartphones.

    Overall, the September 30, 2025 launch marks the beginning of a new chapter in computing history. With Meta Ray-Ban Display, a future where AI agents naturally blend into our daily lives is about to become reality.
