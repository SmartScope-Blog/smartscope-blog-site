Autopilot Stop Conditions
Autopilot stops automatically when any of the following conditions are met. Even in fully autonomous execution, humans always remain in control.
  • ✅
    AI judges task complete Processing ends automatically once the AI determines the configured goal has been achieved.
  • 🚫
    Blocking problem detected Stops when the AI determines it cannot proceed, such as permission errors or unresolvable dependencies.
  • ⌨️
    User interrupts with Ctrl+C Manual interruption is possible at any time. Changes made so far remain in place.
  • 🔢
    --max-autopilot-continues limit reached Set a maximum number of loop iterations to explicitly control cost and execution time.
--max-autopilot-continues simulation
1 50 10
Auto-stop after 10 steps. A good setting for moderate-complexity tasks.
--allow-all flag
ON
Grants access to all tools, paths, and URLs. Recommended for Autopilot.
