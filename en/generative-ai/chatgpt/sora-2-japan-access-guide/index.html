    How to Access Sora from Japan (October 2025 Update)¶

    Quick facts

    Sora is bundled as Sora Turbo inside ChatGPT Plus and ChatGPT Pro at no additional cost.1 OpenAI does not distribute public invite codes. Third‑party “code sales” on social media are scams. Users in Japan can activate Sora from the ChatGPT web app once they hold a valid Plus/Pro subscription—VPNs are not required.

    1. Understand the official release status¶

    • Sora debuted in February 2024 as a research preview restricted to safety testers.
    • Since December 2024, ChatGPT Plus and ChatGPT Pro subscribers receive “Sora Turbo” with no extra fee. OpenAI says tiered pricing for other user types will follow.1
    • Access is tied to your OpenAI account. As long as billing is enabled, Japan-based users can open the same interface as US users.

    Ignore unofficial offers

    OpenAI has not announced “Sora 2” or public invitation codes. Any DM offering paid invites is likely phishing or resale of compromised accounts. Never share payment credentials or one-time passcodes.

    2. Prerequisites for Japan-based users¶

    RequirementDetails
    OpenAI accountAble to sign in to ChatGPT
    PlanChatGPT Plus (individual) or ChatGPT Pro (team/business)
    Payment methodCredit/debit card that supports international billing
    PlatformLatest Chrome/Edge/Safari or the ChatGPT desktop app
    LanguagePrompts can be written in Japanese or English

    How to upgrade if you are on the free tier

    1. Visit https://chat.openai.com and sign in.
    2. Open the left sidebar → Settings → Plans.
    3. Choose ChatGPT Plus or ChatGPT Pro and complete the payment flow.

    3. Enable and launch Sora Turbo¶

    1. Sign in to ChatGPT using your Plus/Pro account.
    2. Open Labs / Video: In the sidebar select Labs (or Explore GPTs), then choose Sora Turbo under the Video category. Accept any first-use terms.
    3. Describe your video: Enter a concise prompt (Japanese supported) and click Generate. Rendering typically takes from several seconds to a few minutes.
    4. Download or share: Export the result as MP4. Review OpenAI’s usage policy before commercial distribution.

    Sora tab not showing up?

    Confirm that your subscription is active, refresh the page, and try a different browser. Managed enterprise tenants may disable Labs—check with your workspace admin.

    4. Prompt design best practices¶

    Tokyo skyline at dusk, cinematic tracking shot after rainfall. Streets glistening with reflections, pedestrians carrying transparent umbrellas. Shot on a virtual 35mm lens, ISO 200, shallow depth of field.
    • Break prompts into clauses covering subject, camera motion, lighting, and style.
    • Mention camera parameters (lens, aperture, ISO) to lock the look.
    • Start with shorter durations (5‑10 seconds) before scaling to longer clips.

    5. Security and compliance checklist¶

    • Uploaded assets: Keep personal photos or internal documents out of prompts unless cleared by your organisation’s policy.
    • Licensing: Review OpenAI’s Terms of Use before commercial deployment.
    • Audit trail: Plus/Pro sessions are stored in the cloud. Establish a retention policy for sensitive projects.

    6. Troubleshooting¶

    IssueQuick fix
    Sora Turbo tile missingVerify subscription status, clear cache, switch browsers, or update the desktop app.
    Generation fails mid-waySimplify the prompt, reduce requested duration, and retry outside peak hours.
    Output quality is offSpecify camera movement, lighting, and subject details explicitly; iterate with short clips.
    Enterprise account blockedWorkspace admins may disable Labs. Request policy changes or use an individual Plus plan.

    7. FAQ¶

    Can I run Sora on the mobile ChatGPT app?
    Web and desktop provide the most reliable access. If the mobile app lacks the Video tab, switch to the browser version.

    Are there hidden usage fees?
    No. Sora Turbo is included in the monthly Plus/Pro subscription, and OpenAI states that dedicated pricing will be announced later.1

    Do Japanese prompts work well?
    Yes. For precise cinematic directions you may prefer English, but multilingual prompts are supported.
