    ChatGPT Pulse Complete Guide: Transform Your Morning with AI-Curated Cards¶

    What is ChatGPT Pulse?¶

    ChatGPT Pulse is a revolutionary feature that delivers personalized information cards every morning. Through asynchronous research during the night, AI analyzes your conversation history and interests to generate personalized cards delivered the next morning.

    Availability (Preview launched September 25, 2025)

    • Target: ChatGPT Pro accounts ($20/month)
    • Platform: iOS/Android mobile apps only (Web/Desktop not supported)
    • Required Settings: Memory (saved memories) and Chat History references must be ON
    • Future Rollout: Planned phased expansion to Plus users (timing TBD)

    Traditional vs. Pulse-Powered Mornings¶

    Traditional Morning Routine¶

    • News checking: 10 minutes
    • Email review: 10 minutes
    • Calendar check: 5 minutes
    • Weather & traffic: 5 minutes
    • Total: ~30 minutes

    Morning with Pulse¶

    • Card review: 5 minutes complete
    • All information in one place
    • Personalized priority ranking

    Setup Guide¶

    Step 1: App Preparation¶

    1. Update iOS/Android ChatGPT app to latest version
2. Upgrade to Pro account
3. In Settings → Personalization, enable:
   - Reference saved memories: ON (Required)
   - Reference chat history: ON (Required)
   - Show Pulse in new chats: ON

    Step 2: Connector Configuration (Optional)¶

    External App Integration:
  Gmail: Default OFF (Enable "Allow proactive activity" if needed)
  Google Calendar: Default OFF (Enable "Allow proactive activity" if needed)

Note: Connected content is not used for model training

    Step 3: Customization¶

    Configure interests via Curate feature (set by 10 PM local time for next morning's reflection): - "Daily K-drama updates please" - "Political news weekly summary is enough" - "Skip diet content for now"

    Operational Point

    Curate settings made by 10 PM are aimed to be reflected in the next morning's cards

    Enterprise Notice

    • Plan Support: Currently Pro individual plans only (Enterprise/Team/Edu not supported)
    • Data Handling: Gmail/Calendar content not used for model training
    • Governance: Maintain default OFF, recommend phased department pilot deployment
    • Shutdown Method: Disable references in Settings → Personalization

    Troubleshooting¶

    Common Issues and Solutions

    Cards not arriving - Check notification settings - Restart the app - Verify Pro account status

    Too many irrelevant cards - Reconfigure interests in Curate - Use 👎 to accelerate learning - Reset memory (last resort)
